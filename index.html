Top Places to Visit in Vizag

Ramakrishna Beach

Ramakrishna Beach, or RK Beach, is one of Vizag's most popular spots for enjoying the sunrise, relaxing, and experiencing vibrant seaside activities.

Kailasagiri Hill Park

This scenic hilltop park offers panoramic views of the city and sea, and is adorned with statues of Lord Shiva and Parvati, making it a serene and spiritual spot.

INS Kurusura Submarine Museum

The first submarine museum in Asia, INS Kurusura offers a glimpse into the life of navy personnel and the functioning of a submarine.

Araku Valley

Known as Andhra Pradesh's Ooty, Araku Valley is a stunning hill station with coffee plantations, waterfalls, and breathtaking natural scenery.
